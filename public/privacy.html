Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Flight Price Tracker ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension and website.

2. What Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email Address: When you create an account or upgrade to Pro
  • Flight Search Data: Airport codes, dates, number of passengers, cabin class, and stops preference
  • Google Flights URLs: The exact search links you track
  • Payment Information: Processed by Lemon Squeezy (we never see credit card details)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Price history for tracked flights
  • Timestamps of price checks
  • Extension usage data (how many flights tracked, features used)
  • Browser version and operating system (for compatibility)

Information NOT Collected

  • Credit card or payment details (handled by Lemon Squeezy)
  • Browsing history outside of Google Flights
  • Personal location data
  • Passwords (Chrome Auth handles authentication)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Monitor flight prices and send price drop alerts via email
  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify Pro subscription status
  • Send you service-related notifications
  • Improve the extension (feature requests, bug fixes)
  • Comply with legal obligations

We use your data ONLY for the above purposes. We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your email with airlines or travel companies
  • Use your data for targeted advertising
  • Track you across the web

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services to operate Flight Price Tracker:

Supabase (Database)

  • Stores user accounts, flight data, and price history
  • Encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Privacy Policy: supabase.com/privacy

Resend (Email)

  • Sends price alert emails
  • Does not store emails or create profiles
  • Privacy Policy: resend.com/privacy

Lemon Squeezy (Payments)

  • Processes Pro subscriptions
  • Handles all payment information securely
  • Privacy Policy: lemonsqueezy.com/privacy

Google Flights (Price Data)

  • We scrape public flight prices from Google Flights
  • No personal data is sent to Google from our extension

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted)
  • Database encryption at rest (Supabase)
  • No passwords stored (Supabase Auth handles securely)
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No access logs shared externally

6. Your Data Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request complete account deletion
  • Portability: Export your data in standard format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from price alerts anytime

To exercise these rights, email us at support@flightpricetracker.com

7. Email Preferences

You can manage email preferences by:

  • Clicking "Unsubscribe" at the bottom of any price alert email
  • Removing flights from tracking in the extension
  • Contacting us at support@flightpricetracker.com

8. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backups: Deleted data may exist in backups for 90 days
  • Email logs: Resend retains for 30 days, then deleted

9. Cookies & Local Storage

Our extension uses:

  • Chrome Local Storage: Stores your tracked flights (on your device only)
  • No cookies: We don't use cookies
  • No tracking pixels: We don't track you across sites

10. Children's Privacy

Flight Price Tracker is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of data collection from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

11. International Users

Flight Price Tracker is available globally. If you're in the EU, your data is protected under GDPR. If you're in California, your data is protected under CCPA. We comply with all applicable privacy laws.

12. Policy Changes

We may update this policy as needed. When we do, we'll:

  • Update the "Last updated" date above
  • Notify you via email if changes are material
  • Request consent if required by law

13. Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at:

  • Email: support@flightpricetracker.com
  • Website: flightpricetracker.com

Your privacy is our priority. If you don't feel comfortable with how we handle your data, we respect your decision not to use Flight Price Tracker.
